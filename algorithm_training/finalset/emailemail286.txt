Join us in our catered dinner this fall, featuring Professor Amartya Sen
and Professor Emma Rothschild! Professor Sen was awarded the 1998 Nobel
Memorial Prize in Economics for his contributions to welfare
economics and social choice theory. His research has ranged over social
choice theory, economic theory, ethics and political philosophy, welfare
economics, theory of measurement, decision theory, development economics,
public health, and gender studies. Professor Rothschild's work focuses on 18th
century history, especially the history of economic thought and economic
history, and is currently the Director of the Joint Center for History and
Economics at Harvard.
