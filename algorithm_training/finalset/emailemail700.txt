[Canaday-list] TOMORROW: Lunch with Professor Filipe Campante
Brazil in 2012: Discussion with Filipe Campante
A conversation over Brazilian food with:

 Filipe Campante, an associate professor of public policy at the
Harvard Kennedy
School, focuses on political economy and economic development. Born and
raised in Rio de Janiero, Brazil, Professor Campante will speak about Brazil's
growing role in the world economy. By discussing the constraints facing
governments and politicians, Campante will discuss governance and international
relations particularly given the upcoming World Cup and Olympic Games.

Tuesday, December 4, 12:00 pm
Eliot Memorial Dining Room
