The Harvard Undergraduate Economics Association presents:


*Economics Peer Mentorship Program

The Harvard Undergraduate Economics Association is excited to invite you to
apply to be a peer mentor in the *Economics Mentorship Program*! You will
interact with undergraduates who show an interest in Economics and help
them navigate the concentration by mingling at various "Mentorship Mixers"
throughout the year. This is a truly unique leadership opportunity to
become more involved with the Economics Department.
All Harvard undergraduates, preferably juniors and seniors, are invited to
apply.
