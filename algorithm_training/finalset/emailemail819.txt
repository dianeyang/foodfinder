*Apply to join the 2013 Harvard Student Art Show Executive Board!*

The Harvard Student Art Show is an independent event held annually each
spring which displays and sells the artwork of students from Harvard
College and the Harvard graduate schools. We are looking to recruit
students with diverse interests and concentrations. Attached are the
executive board positions for which we are accepting applications.
