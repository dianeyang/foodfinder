ABHW Action Committee Interest Form


If you're interested in being a part of the ABHW Action committee and  
helping plan or staff events with AAC this spring, please fill out this  
interest form! The ABHW Action Committee (AAC) is a coalition of Black  
Harvard women created to raise awareness and facilitate activism toward  
issues that directly affect Blacks, and women. The AAC keeps ABHW abreast  
of pertinent political and social happenings in the women and black  
communities by facilitating general meetings, events, and forums that speak  
to the action-related concerns of the organization.
