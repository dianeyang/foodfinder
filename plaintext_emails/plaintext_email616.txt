> Hi Everyone,

> I hope you are all having great breaks.  I've participated in Relay for
> Life since my freshmen year here. It's a really great event. It's not a
> race. It's more like an overnight party where you get to hang with friends
> and donate to a great cause. It basically begins with an opening ceremony
> to kickoff events. Throughout the night participants have the option of
> walking around Gordon track (right across the river) or participating in
> night activities. It's really chill, and you can come and go. Or you can
> stay at the event all night. A ton of people camp out for the entire night.
>
> The Relay for Life committee at Harvard (
> http://main.acsevents.org/goto/HarvardBSA) works really hard to make it
> fun. I've provided a montage of .jpgs and .gifs to convey what it means to
> me.
>
>
> There are a ton of night time activities. While there is a formal time to
> walk in honor of cancer survivors, you are there to have fun!
>

> every year.
